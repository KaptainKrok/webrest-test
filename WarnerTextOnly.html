Coastal Escape

Coastal Village on the Isle of Wight

Literally yards away from the Solent, the views and coastal walks are spectacular. The Waterfront Lounge and Terrace is the perfect spot to soak up the sun and watch the world sail by.

This is the kind of stay that feels immediately open, bright and breezy — a place where the coast sets the tone for the whole experience.

Spend the day wandering scenic paths, pausing at the waterfront, or enjoying a little activity before easing into a relaxed evening atmosphere.

Whether you are here for sea air, leisure facilities or a sociable break, Norton Grange balances comfort, entertainment and location beautifully.
